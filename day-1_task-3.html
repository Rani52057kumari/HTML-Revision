BTS

Intoduction

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan) is a globally renowned seven-member South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. Since their debut in 2013, they have transcended language and cultural barriers, becoming one of the most successful and influential musical acts in history through their empowering lyrics and dedicated fanbase.

History

BTS—composed of RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook—debuted in 2013 under the small, underdog label Big Hit Entertainment. Starting with a heavy hip-hop influence and lyrics tackling societal pressures, they grew from a broke, mocked rookie group into the biggest pop act in history, spearheading the global K-pop wave
